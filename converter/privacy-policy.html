Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Any-to-Any Converter is a file conversion tool that processes files entirely on your device. We designed it with privacy as a core principle. Your files never leave your computer.

What data is stored

Any-to-Any Converter stores data locally on your device using browser storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly download or share converted files. Examples include:

  • Extension preferences and settings
  • Conversion history (optional, stored locally only)
  • Usage quotas (for free tier limits)
  • Premium status verification data

File processing

All file conversions happen locally in your browser. We do not upload, transmit, or store your files on any server. This means:

  • Your files stay on your device
  • No third-party has access to your content
  • Conversions work offline (after extension is loaded)
  • Maximum privacy for sensitive documents

Permissions we request and why

  • storage: Save your preferences, history, and quota locally
  • offscreen: Process file conversions in the background
  • downloads: Save converted files to your device
  • identity: Google Sign-In for premium account verification

These permissions are used solely to provide file conversion functionality. We do not send your files to external servers.

Premium accounts

If you purchase Any-to-Any Converter Premium, we store your email address and purchase information to verify your subscription status. This data is processed securely via our payment provider (Razorpay) and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

Any-to-Any Converter does not use third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking pixels. The only external services we interact with are:

  • Google Sign-In: For account authentication (optional, only for premium features)
  • Razorpay: For payment processing (only when making a purchase)

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do NOT collect or transmit your files
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • We do NOT sell any user data
  • We do NOT use advertising or tracking pixels

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes occur.
