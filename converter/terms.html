Description of service

Any-to-Any Converter is a browser extension that converts files between various formats including images, documents, PDFs, QR codes, and more. All processing happens locally on your device. It offers both free and premium features.

Free vs Premium

The free tier includes:

  • Limited daily conversions (10 image, 5 PDF, 5 document conversions)
  • Basic conversion types
  • 10MB file size limit

Premium tier includes:

  • Unlimited conversions
  • Video & Audio conversions
  • Batch processing (up to 20 files)
  • 100MB file size limit
  • All 200+ conversion types
  • Priority support
