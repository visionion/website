Refund Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Digital product

Any-to-Any Converter Premium is a digital product (browser extension upgrade with lifetime access). Due to the nature of digital products, we generally do not offer automatic refunds after purchase.

14-Day satisfaction guarantee

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If you're unsatisfied with Any-to-Any Converter Premium or experience technical issues that we cannot resolve, you may request a refund within 14 days of your purchase.

Eligible refund reasons

  • Technical issues preventing the extension from working
  • Features not working as described
  • Accidental duplicate purchases
  • Extension incompatibility with your browser (after troubleshooting)

Non-eligible refund reasons

  • Change of mind after the 14-day period
  • Not reading the feature description before purchase
  • Issues unrelated to the extension (e.g., file format not supported as listed)
  • Requests made after 14 days from purchase

How to request a refund

To request a refund, please:

  • Email us at info@visionion.dev
  • Include your order/transaction ID (from Razorpay receipt)
  • Include the email address used for purchase
  • Describe the issue you encountered
  • Include screenshots if applicable

Processing time

We will review refund requests within 2 business days. Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.

Partial refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds or credits at our discretion, particularly if significant use of premium features occurred before the refund request.

Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or card provider without first contacting us, your account may be suspended and you may be banned from future purchases.

Contact

For any refund-related questions, email us at info@visionion.dev. We're here to help!
