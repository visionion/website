Cancellation & Refund

Last updated: December 15, 2025

One-time purchases

Our browser extensions (FocusGuard, Snapp) offer lifetime access with a one-time payment. Since there's no recurring billing, there's nothing to cancel.

Subscription products

If you have a subscription (e.g., EnduroTrack or future native apps), you can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Refund requests

If you're unhappy with a purchase or experience issues we can't resolve, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a refund review.

  • Email: info@visionion.dev
  • Include your order/transaction ID
  • Describe the issue

Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.

Contact

Questions? Email us at info@visionion.dev.
