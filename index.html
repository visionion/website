Who We Are

Visionion is an independent software studio dedicated to building thoughtful, privacy-respecting tools that help people work smarter and live better. We believe technology should serve you — not the other way around. That's why every product we create is designed with intention, focusing on simplicity, performance, and respect for your time and data.

Our mission is straightforward: create useful software that solves real problems without unnecessary complexity or invasive tracking. Whether you're trying to stay focused during deep work, capture and annotate screenshots efficiently, or understand your health trends, our apps are built to help you accomplish your goals with minimal friction.

We take a privacy-first approach to everything we build. Your data stays on your device whenever possible, and we never sell or share your information with third parties. We believe in transparent business models — our apps offer generous free tiers with optional premium features for users who want more.

What We Offer

FocusGuard is our productivity browser extension that helps you block distracting websites and build better browsing habits. Set up custom blocklists, schedule focus sessions, and track your progress over time. It's perfect for students, remote workers, and anyone looking to reclaim their attention from endless scrolling.

Snapp is a powerful screenshot tool that goes beyond simple captures. Take full-page screenshots, annotate with shapes and text, blur sensitive information, and export to multiple formats including PDF. It's the ideal tool for designers, developers, and professionals who need to communicate visually.

Any-to-Any Converter is our universal file conversion tool. Convert between 200+ file formats including images, PDFs, documents, QR codes, video, and audio — all processed locally on your device. No uploads, no waiting, complete privacy.

EnduroTrack is our upcoming iOS app for understanding your health and wellness trends. Using data from Apple Health, it provides actionable insights about your activity, recovery, and readiness — all processed locally on your device for maximum privacy.

Xly is our intentional X (Twitter) client for Mac. It gives you full access to DMs and notifications while limiting addictive timeline browsing to 5 minutes per hour. With zero servers, zero tracking, and zero data collection, it's the most private way to use X.

Voxly is our voice-to-text app for Mac, powered by OpenAI's Whisper running 100% locally. Press a hotkey, speak, and watch your words appear instantly. No cloud, no servers, no data collection — your voice never leaves your Mac.

We're always working on new ideas and improvements. Subscribe to our newsletter below to stay updated on new releases, feature updates, and exclusive early access opportunities.
