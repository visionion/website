EnduroTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

EnduroTrack is a health insights app that helps you understand your wellness and activity trends. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your health data.

Health data

EnduroTrack accesses health data from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data is processed locally on your device to generate insights. We do not upload your health data to our servers.

  • Heart rate and HRV data
  • Sleep analysis
  • Activity and workout data
  • Recovery metrics

What we store

EnduroTrack stores the following data:

  • On your device: Your health insights, preferences, and settings
  • On our servers: Your email address and subscription status (for premium features)

Premium accounts

If you subscribe to EnduroTrack Premium, we store your Apple ID email and subscription information to manage your subscription. This data is processed securely and is not shared with third parties for marketing.

Third-party services

EnduroTrack does not use third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking pixels. We do not sell or share your health data with anyone.

Data security

Your health data remains on your device and is protected by iOS security measures. Any data transmitted to our servers (e.g., subscription info) is encrypted in transit.

Your rights

You can revoke EnduroTrack's access to your health data at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy > Health. You can also request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

Not medical advice

EnduroTrack provides informational insights only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, or emergency services.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes occur.
